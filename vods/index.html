start.gg VOD Splitter

Split a tournament VOD into per-set clips, right in your browser

1. Load the VOD

Pick the local recording. It stays on your machine — nothing is uploaded. Everything (previews and the actual cutting) runs in your browser.

No file loaded.

2. Get the sets

Paste a start.gg event to pull its completed sets and their times, then pick the station this VOD recorded. Or skip this and add clips by hand below.

3. Review & cut

Check each clip's start/end frame before cutting. Times are editable — nudge them and re-preview. Cutting uses stream copy (-c copy), so it's fast and lossless, and lands on the nearest keyframe.
